Cash on Delivery for e-commerce website stress free

You can offer your customers the flexibility to skip online payment during checkout and instead pay for their order at the time of delivery. This convenient Cash on Delivery (COD) option enhances the shopping experience for users who prefer to pay in person, helping you reach a wider audience and potentially increase your sales.
